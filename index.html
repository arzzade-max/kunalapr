Who We Are

Building a Safe Future Together

Sabka Malik Ek Pratishthan, operating as Saiashraya, is a charitable organization rooted in Maharashtra that believes every child deserves love, safety, and opportunity — regardless of their circumstances.

We provide a nurturing home for orphans, abandoned children, and those from vulnerable backgrounds. Guided by the timeless wisdom of Sabka Malik Ek — "One God for All" — we work to restore dignity, instil values, and empower children to build a responsible, independent future.
